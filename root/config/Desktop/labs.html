Google Lens

Any Website
  1. Click the three dots in the top right corner of Chrome (just below the close button).
  2. Select "Search with Google Lens".
  3. Select what you want Gemini to answer.
  4. Optional: Pin Google Lens for easier access.

Shutdown

Chromebooks
  1. For Chromebooks, pressing Power + Refresh automatically cuts power to the computer.
  2. Sliding a strong magnet down the side of it triggers the lid sensor, but can damage components.
  3. Pressing Ctrl + Shift + Q twice rapidly also safely shuts down the Chromebook.

Shortcuts

Any Website
  1. Hit Ctrl + W to quickly close your current active tab.
  2. Hit Ctrl + Shift + W to close the entire Chrome window and all tabs.
  3. Use Ctrl + Tab to cycle forward between your open tabs.
  4. Use Alt + Tab to switch between active OS windows.
  5. Use Ctrl + Shift + Del to open the clear browsing data menu.
