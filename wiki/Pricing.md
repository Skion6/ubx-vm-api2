# Pricing

## Free Tier

- Limited to **10 concurrent VMs** at a time
- Each VM is limited to **60 minutes** of usage
- If you exceed the 10 VM limit, you must wait until someone using your site stops using Xcloud before starting a new VM
- VMs will be deleted after a minute of activity

## Xcloud+

- **$5/month per user**
- Get a dedicated instance that doesn't get deleted unless you cancel
- Guaranteed resources
- Uptime guarantee of 85%

## Partner Program

UBG owners who add Xcloud and are compliant with the full Xcloud standard receive **10% of the sale** as a commission.
